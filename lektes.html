🤝
AI assists. Humans decide.

Lektes is a screening tool, not a decision-maker. It ranks, scores, and surfaces insights — but the final hiring decision always rests with the human. Every score comes with a written justification so HR professionals can evaluate the AI's reasoning, agree or disagree, and make their own informed call. We believe AI should reduce workload, not remove judgment.

🇪🇺
Designed for the EU AI Act

The EU AI Act classifies AI recruitment screening as "high-risk." Core compliance requirements — documentation, bias testing, human oversight, and audit trails — become enforceable on 2 August 2026. Lektes is built with these requirements in mind from day one.
