About Lektes

Built by an engineer,
for HR teams.

Lektes was built to solve a real problem: recruiters spend the majority of their time on the most repetitive part of hiring — manually reading and comparing CVs. Most are screened out in seconds anyway. Lektes automates that first pass so HR teams can focus on what matters: conversations with the right people.

Lektes is part of Nimbus-24, a technology venture focused on building practical AI tools for the European market. It was built and showcased at the LangChain meet-up at The Commons Hub, Brussels in March 2026.
